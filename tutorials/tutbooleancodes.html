Boolean Codes

Boolean codes are fundamental in computer science and digital electronics. They are used in logical operations, decision making, and binary computations. Boolean values include TRUE and FALSE, represented by 1 and 0, respectively.

Boolean Logic

Boolean logic consists of operations such as AND, OR, and NOT. These operations help in constructing logical statements and circuits in programming and electronics.
